Fried Chicken

Description

This recipe is a southern staple. Fried chicken is simple and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 4lbs of chicken
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Place flour in large plastic bag. Season the flour with paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Dip chicken pieces in buttermilk, then a few at a time, put them in the bag with flour, seal the bag and shake to coat well.
  3. Place the chicken on tray and let it sit until the flour is a paste like consistency.
  4. Fill a cast iron skillet with oil.
  5. Fry the chicken until it reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees farenheit.
  6. Let chicken rest on paper towels to darin oil.
